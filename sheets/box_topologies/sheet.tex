\section*{Definition}

Given a family of topological spaces $\mathcal{F}  = \set{(X_\alpha , \mathcal{T} _\alpha }_{\alpha  \in I}$, the \t{box topology} of $\mathcal{F} $ is the topology on $X = \prod_{\alpha  \in I} X_\alpha $ generated by the set
\[
\Set*{ \prod_{\alpha  \in I} U_i }{ U_\alpha  \in \mathcal{T} _\alpha  }
\]
