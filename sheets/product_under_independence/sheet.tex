
\section*{Result}

\begin{proposition}
The expectation ofthe product oftwo independentrandom variablesis the product oftheir expectations.
\end{proposition}

\begin{proof}Let $f, g$ be two independent random variables.
We want to show
\[
\E  fg = \E f \E g.
\]\end{proof}
