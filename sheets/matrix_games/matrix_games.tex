\ssection{Definition}

A \ct{matrix game}{}

A \ct{two-player one-shot game}{} is
a sequence of four objects:
a set $A$, $B$ and two matrices
$C$ and $D$.
WE call $C$ and $D$ the
\ct{pay-off matrices}{}.
