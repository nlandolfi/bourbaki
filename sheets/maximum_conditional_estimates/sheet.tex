\ssection{Definition}

Suppose $x$
A \t{maximum conditional estimate} for $x: \Omega \to \R^n$ given $y: \Omega \to \R^d$

It is also called the \t{maximum a posteriori estimate} or \t{MAP estimate}.


\begin{proposition}
The MAP estimate also maximizes the joint pdf.
\end{proposition}
