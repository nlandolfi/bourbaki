\section*{Definition}

Let $\F  = (F, +, \cdot )$ be a \sheetref{fields}{field}.
A \t{topological field} is a tuple $(\F , \mathcal{T} )$ , where $(F, \mathcal{T} )$ is a topological space and the \sheetref{fields}{field operations}are continuous.
