\section*{Definition}

Let $K \subset \R ^n$ be a proper cone.
Define a relation $\preceq_K \subset \R ^n \times \R ^n$ by $(x, y) \in \preceq_K$ if $y - x \in K$.
We call $\preceq_K$ the \t{cone-ordering} (or \t{generalized inequality}) of $K$.
