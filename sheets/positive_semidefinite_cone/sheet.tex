
\section*{Why}

The set of positive semidefinite matrices has an attractive geometric structure.

\section*{Main result}

\begin{proposition}
$\mathbfsf{S} _+^d$ is a convex, pointed, closed cone with interior $S_{++}^d$ relative to $\mathbfsf{S} ^d$.\footnote{Future editions will contain a proof.}
\end{proposition}

The cone of positive definite matrices is open.
