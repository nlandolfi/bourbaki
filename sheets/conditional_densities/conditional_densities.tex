\ssection{Notation}


Let $f: \R^d \to \R$ a density.
For $i, j = 1, \dots, d$ and
$i \neq j$,
let $f_{i \mid j}: \R^2 \to \R$
satisfy
\[
f_{ij}(\xi, \gamma) = f_{i \mid j}(\xi, \gamma)f_j(\gamma)
\]
for $\xi, \gamma \in \R$.
