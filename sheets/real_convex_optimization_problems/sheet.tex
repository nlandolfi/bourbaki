
\section*{Definition}

Suppose $X \subset \R ^d$ and $f: \R ^d \to \R $.
An optimization problem $(X, f)$ is \t{convex} (a \t{convex optimization problem}, \t{convex program}, \t{ordinary convex program}) if $X$ is a convex set and $f$ is a convex function.
