\section*{Why}

Is the sum of two functions integrable?
What is their sum.

\section*{Result}

\begin{proposition}
Suppose $f, g: \R  \to \R $ are integrable.
Then the function $f+g$ is integrable and
\[
\int f + g = \int f + \int g
\]
\end{proposition}
