\ssection{Why}

We extend the notion of a square number to the reals.

\ssection{Definition}

A number $a \in \R$ is \t{square} if there exists $b \in \R$ so that $b^2 = a$.\footnote{Future editions may collapse this sheet with \sheetref{real_square_roots}{Real Square Roots}.}
