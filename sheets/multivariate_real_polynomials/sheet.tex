\section*{Definition}

A \t{multivariate polynomial} is a function $f: \R ^n \to \R $ whose terms are \t{monomials}.
The \t{degree} of a monomial is the sum of the degrees of the each of the variable terms in it.
The \t{degree} of a multvariate polynomial is the largest degree of its terms.
