\section*{Definition}

The \t{space inner product} (or \t{dot product}, \t{scalar product}) of two real vectors $x, y \in \R ^3$ is
    \[
x_1y_1 + x_2y_2 + x_3y_3
    \]
We denote the inner product of $x$ and $y$ by $\ip{x,y}$.
