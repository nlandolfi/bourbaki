Let $A \in \RMat{d}{d}$.
The determinant of $A$ is
\[
  \sum_{\sigma \in S_n} \left( \sgn(\sigma) \prod_{i = 1}^{n} a_{i,\sigma_{i}} \right)
\]
We denote the determinant of $A$ by $\det A$.
