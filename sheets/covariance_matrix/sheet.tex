\section*{Definition}

The covariance matrix of a \sheetref{random_vector}{random vector}$x: \Omega  \to \R ^d$ (on a probability space $(\Omega , \mathcal{F} , \mathbfsf{P} )$) is the matrix whose $i,j$th entry is $\cov(x_i,x_j)$
