\ssection{Why}

The real numbers are
one of the most important
sets in mathematics. So let
us discuss sequences of them.

\ssection{Definition}

A \ct{real sequence}{}
is a sequence in the real
numbers, or a sequence of
real numbers.

\ssection{Notation}

A sequence of real
numbers is a funciton
$a: \N \to \R$.
