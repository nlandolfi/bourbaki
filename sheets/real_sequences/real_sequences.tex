\stitle{Real Sequences}

\ssection{Why}

The real numbers are
one of the most important
of our sets.
Let us discuss sequences of
them.

\ssection{Definition}

A \ct{real sequence}{}
is a sequence in the real
numbers, or a sequence of
real numbers.

\ssection{Notation}

A sequence of real
numbers is a function
$a: \N \to \R$.
