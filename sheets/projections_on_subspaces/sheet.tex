\section*{Why}

Given a vector $x \in \R ^n$ and a subspace $S \subset \R ^n$, what is the closest point in $S$ to $x$.

\section*{Result}

\begin{proposition}
Suppose $U \in \R ^{n \times  k}$ with $U^\top U = I$.
Then
\[
\textstyle
\proj_{U}(x) = UU^\top x
\]
\end{proposition}
