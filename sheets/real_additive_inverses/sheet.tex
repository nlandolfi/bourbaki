\section*{Why}

What is the additive inverse for reals.\footnote{Future editions will expand.}

\section*{Main result}

\begin{proposition}
Let $R \in \R $.
The set $\Set{-r}{r \in R \text{ and } s \not\in R}$ is an additive inverse of $R$ in $\R $.\end{proposition}
\section*{Notation}

We denote the additive inverse of $R \in \R $ by $-R$.
