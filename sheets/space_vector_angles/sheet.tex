\section*{Why}

We generalize the notion of angles in the plane to angles in space.

\section*{Definition}

The \t{angle} (\t{unsigned angle}) between $x \in \R ^3$ and $y \in \R ^3$ is the real number
  \[
\theta  = \angle(x, y) = \cos^{-1}\frac{x^\top  y}{\norm{x}\norm{y}}
  \]
