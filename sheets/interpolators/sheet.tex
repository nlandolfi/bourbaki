
\section*{Definition}

Let $(u^1, v^1), \dots , (u^n, v^n)$ be a paired dataset in $\mathcal{U}  \times  \mathcal{V} $.
An \t{interpolator} is a predictor $f: \mathcal{U}  \to \mathcal{V} $ which satisifes for which
\[
f(u^i) = u^i
\]
for each $i = 1 , \dots , n$.
In this case we say that the predictor $f$ \t{interpolates} the dataset.
