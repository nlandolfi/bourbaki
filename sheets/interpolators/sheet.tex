\ssection{Definition}

Let $(a^1, b^1), \dots, (a^n, b^n)$ be a paired dataset in $A \times B$.
An \t{interpolator} is a predictor $f: A \to B$ which satisifes
\[
  f(a^i) = b^i
\]
for each $i = 1 , \dots, n$.
In this case we say that the predictor $f$ \t{interpolates} the dataset.
