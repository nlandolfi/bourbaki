\ssection{Definition}

Let $(u^1, v^1), \dots, (u^n, v^n)$ be a paired dataset in $\CU \times \CV$.
An \t{interpolator} is a predictor $f: \CU \to \CV$ which satisifes for which
  \[
f(u^i) = u^i
  \]
for each $i = 1 , \dots, n$.
In this case we say that the predictor $f$ \t{interpolates} the dataset.
