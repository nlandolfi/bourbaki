\ssection{Why}

What is the identity element of natural multiplication?

\begin{proposition}
  1 is the identity element of $\omega$ under $\cdot$.
\end{proposition}
\begin{proof}
  By definition $1 \cdot n = n$ (see \sheetref{natural_products}{Natural Products}).
\end{proof}
