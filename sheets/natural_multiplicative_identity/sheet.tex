\section*{Why}

What is the identity element of natural multiplication?

\begin{proposition}
1 is the identity element of $\omega $ under $\cdot $.
\begin{proof}By definition $1 \cdot  n = n$ (see \sheetref{natural\_products}{Natural Products}).\end{proof}\end{proposition}
