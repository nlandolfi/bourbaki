\stitle{Normal Correlation}

\ssection{Why}

What is the correlation between
components of a normal density.

\ssection{Result}

\begin{prop}
Let $g \sim \normal{\mu}{\Sigma}$.
Then the correlation between $i$ and $j$
is
\[
  \frac{\Sigma_{ij}}{\sqrt{\Sigma_{ii}\Sigma_{jj}}}
\]
\end{prop}
