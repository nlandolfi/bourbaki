
\section*{Why}

What is the correlation between components of a normal density.

\section*{Result}

\begin{proposition}
Let $g \sim \normal{\mu }{\Sigma }$.
Then the correlation between the $i$th and $j$th component random variables is
\[
\frac{\Sigma _{ij}}{\sqrt{\Sigma _{ii}\Sigma _{jj}}}
\]
\end{proposition}
