\ssection{Why}

Do the natural numbers correspond (in the sense \sheetref{isomorphisms}{Isomorphisms}) to elements of integers.

\ssection{Main Result}

Indeed, the natural numbers correspond to the $Z_+$.

\begin{proposition}
  $(\Z_+, +\mid Z_+)$ and $(\omega, +)$ are isomorphic.
\end{proposition}
\begin{proof}
  The function is $f(n) = \eqc{(n, 0)}$.\footnote{The full account will appear in future editions.}
\end{proof}
