\section*{Why}

Do the natural numbers correspond (in the sense of \sheetref{isomorphisms}{Isomorphisms}) to elements of integers.

\section*{Main result}

Indeed, the natural numbers correspond to the $Z_+$.

\begin{proposition}
$(\Z _{++}, + \mid  \Z _{++})$ and $(\omega , +)$ are isomorphic.
\end{proposition}
