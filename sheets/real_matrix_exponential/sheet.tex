\section*{Definition}

The \t{matrix exponential} of a real matrix $M \in \R ^{n \times  n}$ is the matrix
\[
I + M + \frac{M^2}{2!} + \frac{M^3}{3!} + \cdots.
\]
It is a (nontrivial) fact that this (matrix) power series converges for all $M \in \R ^{n \times  n}$.
