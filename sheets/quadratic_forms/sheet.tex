\section*{Definition}

For $A \in \R ^{n \times n}$ and $x \in \R ^n$,
  \[
x^\top  A x = \sum_{i,j} A_{ij}x_{i}x_{j},
  \]
is a quadratic form.

\section*{Under trace}

Observe that $\tr x^\top  A x = \tr A x x^\top  = \tr xx^\top A$.
