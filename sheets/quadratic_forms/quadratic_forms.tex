\ssubsection{Notation}

Let $A \in \R^{d \times d}$.
Let $x \in \R^d$.
Then $x^T A x$ is a quadratic form.
\[
  x^T A x = \sum_{i,j} A_{ij}x_{i}x_{j}
\]
