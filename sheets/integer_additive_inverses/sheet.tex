\ssection{Why}

What is the additive inverse of $\eqc{(a, b)}$ in the integers?

\ssection{Result}

\begin{proposition}
  The additive inverse of $\eqc{(a, b)} \in \Z$ is $\eqc{(b, A)}$.
\end{proposition}
