\ssection{Definition}

Let $A \in \R^{n \times n}$ be real symmetric.
An \t{lower upper triangular decomposition} of $A$ is a matrix $L \in \R^{n \times n}$ that is lower triangular and satisfies
\[
  A = L\transpose{L}.
\]
This is univerally known as a \t{Cholesky decomposition}.
