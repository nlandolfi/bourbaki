\ssection{Why}

What is the identity element of addition of the natural numbers.

\ssection{Result}

\begin{proposition}
  0 is the identity element of $\omega$ under $+$.
\end{proposition}
\begin{proof}
  By definition $0 + n = n$ (see \sheetref{natural_sums}{Natural Sums}).
\end{proof}
