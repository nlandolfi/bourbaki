\section*{Why}

What is the identity element of addition of the natural numbers.

\section*{Result}

\begin{proposition}
0 is the identity element of $\omega $ under $+$.
\end{proposition}

\begin{proof}By definition $0 + n = n$ (see \sheetref{natural_sums}{Natural Sums}).\end{proof}
