\section*{Definition}

Given a family of topological spaces $\mathcal{F}  = \set{(X_\alpha , \mathcal{T} _\alpha }_{\alpha  \in I}$, the \t{product topology} of $\mathcal{F} $ is the topology on $\prod_{\alpha  \in I} X_\alpha $ generated by the set
\[
\Set*{ p_{\beta }^{-1}(U_\beta )) }{ (\exists \beta )(U_\beta  \in X_{\beta }) }
\]
where $p_\beta : \prod_{\alpha  \in I} X_\alpha  \to X_\beta $ is the \sheetref{direct_products}{$\beta $ coordinate projection.}
Some authorities also use the terminology \t{Tychonoff topology}.
