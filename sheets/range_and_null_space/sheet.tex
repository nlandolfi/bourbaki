\section*{Definition}

The \t{range} of a matrix $A \in \R ^{m \times  n}$ is the set
\[
\Set{y \in \R ^m}{(\exists z \in \R ^n)(y = Az)}.
\]
We often denote this set by $\Set{Az}{z \in \R ^n}$.
It is a subset of $\R ^m$, the output space.

The \t{nullspace} of a matrix $A \in \R ^{m \times  n}$ is the set
\[
\Set{z \in \R ^n}{Az = 0}.
\]
It is a subset of $\R ^n$, the input space.
