\section*{Definition}

Suppose $G$ is a \sheetref{groups}{finite group}with identity 1 and suppose $V$ is a vector space over the field $\C $ of complex numbers.
A \t{linear representation} of $G$ in $V$ is a group homomorphism from $G$ to the general linear group $GL(V)$.
