\section*{Definition}

Suppose $\mathcal{G}  = (G, \circ)$ is a \sheetref{groups}{group}.
A \t{topological group} is a tuple $(\mathcal{G} , \mathcal{T} )$ where $(G, \mathcal{T} )$ is a topological space and the group operations are continuous.
