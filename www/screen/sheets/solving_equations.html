Solving Equations

Why

If I am holding 3 stones in both hands, and I have two of them in my left hand, how many might I have in my right hand? If I am holding fewer than 5 pebbles, how many might I have in my right hand?

Overview

An equation is an assertion that two objects are the same. An indeterminate equation is one in which some value is unknown. For example, I am asserting above that I have three pebbles and that I have two in my left hand.
