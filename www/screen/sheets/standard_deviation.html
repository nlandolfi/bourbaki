Definition

The standard deviation of an integrable real-valued random variable is the square root of its variance.

Notation

Let $f$ be an integrable real-valued random variable. Denote the standard deviation of $f$ by $\std(f)$. We have defined it \[ \std(f) = \sqrt{\var(f)}. \]
