Definition

The expectation of a real-valued random variable defined on a probability space is its integral with respect to the probability measure.

Notation

Let $(X, \SA, \mu)$ be a probability space and $f$ be a random variable. We denote the expectation of $f$ by $\Expect f$. We defined it by \[ \Expect f = \int f d\mu. \]
