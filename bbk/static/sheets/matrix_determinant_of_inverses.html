\begin{prop}

The determinant of an invertible square real matrix is the multiplicative inverse of the determinant of the matrix.

\begin{proof}

Let $A \in \RMat{n}{n}$ be invertible. We want to show that $$ \detp{\inv{A}} = \invp{\det A}.$$

\end{proof}

\end{prop}
