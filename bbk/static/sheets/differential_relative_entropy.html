1. Why

We want relative entropy for densities.

2. What

The differential relative entropy of a second density with respect to the first density is the cross entropy of the second with respect to the first less the differential entropy of the first.

2.1 Notation

Let $f$ and $g$ be densities. The relative entropy of $f$ with respect to $g$ is \[ h(g, f) = h(f) \]
