1. Why

What is the differential mutual information between two components of a multivariate normal.

2. Result

\begin{prop} Let $g \sim \normal{\mu}{\Sigma}$ Then the differential entropy of g is \[ \frac{1}{2}\ln\left((2\pi e)^d \det\Sigma\right) \] \end{prop}
