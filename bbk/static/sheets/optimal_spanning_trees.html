1. Why

Trees have the fewest edges of any connected graph. Among spanning trees of a weighted graph, can we find the one for which the sum of weights on the edges is minimal.

2. Why

A \t{minimal spanning tree} is a spanning tree whose weight is minimal among all spanning trees. A \t{maximal spanning tree} is a spanning tree whose weight is maximal among all spanning trees.
