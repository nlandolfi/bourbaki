Cones

Definition

A cone is a subset of $n$-dimensional space which is closed under positive scalar multiplication. A halfline or ray is a point and all positive scalar multiples of that point. A cone is a union of halflines. The origin may or may not be included.

\section*{Definition}

A \t{cone} is a subset of $n$-dimensional space which is closed under positive scalar multiplication. A \t{halfline} or \t{ray} is a point and all positive scalar multiples of that point. A cone is a union of halflines. The origin may or may not be included.
