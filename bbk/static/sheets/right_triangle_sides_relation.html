1. Why

How does constraining a triangle to be a right angle constrain the lengths of its sides?

2. Result

\begin{proposition} Let $a$ and $b$ be the lengths of the sides of a right triangle and $c$ be the length of the hypotenuse. Then \[ a^2 + b^2 = c^2. \] \end{proposition} The result is called \t{Pythagorean theorem} and the equation is called the \t{Pythagorean equation}.
