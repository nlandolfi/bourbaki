\section{Why}

Of course matrices of real numbers are the most important of all.

1. What

A real-valued matrix or matrix of real numbers or \ct{matrix} is a matrix in the real numbers.

1.1 Notation

We denote the set $n$ by $m$ real-valued matrices by $\RMat{n}{m}$.
