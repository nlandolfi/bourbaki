1. Why

We want a simple example of modeling an uncertain outcome.

2. Model

Consider flipping a coin. The coin has two sides. When we flip the coin, it lands heads or tails.

Let us use the set of outcomes $\set{0, 1}$. If the coin lands tails, we will say that outcome 0 has occurred. If the coin lands heads, we will say that outcome 1 has occurred.
