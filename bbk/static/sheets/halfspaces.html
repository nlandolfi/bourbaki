2. Definition

For a nonzero $b \in \R^n$ and $\beta \in \R$ the sets $$ \Set*{x \in \R^n}{\ip{x}{b} \leq \beta}, \quad \Set*{x \in \R^n}{\ip{x}{b} \geq \beta}, $$ are \t{closed halfspaces} and the sets $$ \Set*{x \in \R^n}{\ip{x}{b} \leq \beta}, \quad \Set*{x \in \R^n}{\ip{x}{b} \geq \beta}, $$ are \t{open halfspaces}.

Each of these is nonempty and convex. As with hyperplanes, the same four sets appear if one uses $\lambda \beta$ and $\lambda b$ above, so the halfspaces depend only on the hyperplane $H = \Set*{x}{\ip{x}{b} = \beta}$.
