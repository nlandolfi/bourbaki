1. Why

What is the differential mutual information between two components of a multivariate normal.

2. Result

\begin{prop} Let $g \sim \normal{\mu}{\Sigma}$. Then the mutual information between component $i$ and component $j$ is \[ -\frac{1}{2}\ln(1 - \rho_{ij}^2) \] where $\rho_{ij}$ is the correlation between components $i$ and $j$. \end{prop}
