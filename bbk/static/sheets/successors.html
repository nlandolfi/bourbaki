2. Definition

The \t{successor} of a set is the union of the set with the singleton whose element is the set.

2.1 Notation

Let $x$ be a set. We denote the successor of $x$ by $\ssuc{x}$. We defined it by $$ \ssuc{x} := x \union \set{x} $$
