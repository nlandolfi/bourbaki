\section*{Definition}

Given a distribution $p: \Omega \to \R $, the \t{cumulative distribution} (or \t{cumulative distribution function}, \t{cdf}) of a random variable $x: \Omega \to \R $ is the function $F: \R \to \R $ defined by \[ F(t) = \mathbfsf{P} (x \leq t), \] for all $t \in \R $.

\subsection*{Properties}

The cumulative distribution of any random variable is \begin{enumerate} \item \t{piecewise constant} and \item \t{right continuous}. \end{enumerate}
