1. Why

We want a simple example of modeling an uncertain outcome. We consider rolling a die.

2. Model

The die has six sides. When we roll the die, one of the six sides is facing up. Let us model this uncertain outcome with the set of outcomes $\set{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}$.
