\begin{prop}

The determinant of a product of two real matrices is the product of the determinant of each matrix. \begin{proof}

Let $A \in \RMat{n}{p}$ and $B \in \RMat{p}{n}$. We want to show that \[\detp{AB} = \detp{A}\detp{B}.\]\footnote{Future editions will include a proof.}

\end{proof}

\end{prop}
