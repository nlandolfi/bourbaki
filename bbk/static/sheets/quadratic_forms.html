2. Definition

The \t{quadratic form} of a square matrix $A$ with a vector $x$ is the value $\transpose{x}Ax$.

2.1 Notation

Let $\F$ be a field and $A \in \Mat{\F}{m}{m}$. For a vector $x \in \R^d$, A quadratic for in $A$ Let $x \in \R^d$. Then $x^T A x$ is a quadratic form. \[ x^T A x = \sum_{i,j} A_{ij}x_{i}x_{j} \]
