1. Why

They are an example of a chordal graph. They generalize trees.

2. Definition

A \t{$k$-tree} is an undirected graph with at least $k$ vertices. The only $k$-tree with $k$ vertices is the complete graph.

3. Chordality

\begin{prop}

All $k$-trees are chordal.

\begin{proof}

Induction on $k$-tree with $k$ vertices.

\end{proof}

\end{prop}
