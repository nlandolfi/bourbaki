2. Definition

Two vectors in an inner product space are \t{orthogonal} if their inner product is zero. An \t{orthogonal family of vectors} in an inner product space is a family of vectors for which distinct family members are orthogonal. An \t{orthonormal family of vectors} in an inner product space is a family of vectors with unit norm.
