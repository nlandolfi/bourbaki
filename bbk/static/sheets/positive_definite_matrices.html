\section*{Definition}

Let $A \in \mathbfsf{S} ^n$ (i.e., $A \in \R ^{n \times n}$ and symmetric). $A$ is \t{positive definite} if, for all $x \neq 0 \in \R ^{d}$, \[ x^\top A x > 0. \] $A$ is \t{positive semidefinite} (or \t{nonnegative definite}) if, for all $x \in \R ^{d}$ \[ x^\top A x \geq 0. \]

\subsection*{Notation}

We denote the set of positive definite $d$ by $d$ matrices by $\mathbfsf{S} _{++}^d$. We denote the set of positive semidefinite $d$ by $d$ matrices by $\mathbfsf{S} _{+}^d$.
