1. Why

We provide a principle for density selection based on maximizing the probability of the dataset.

2. Definition

Let $(x^1, \dots, x^n)$ be a dataset in $\R^d$.

The principle of maximum likelihood says to solve: \[ \maximization{f \text{ a density}}{\product_{k = 1}^{n} f(x^k)} \] We call a solution of the above problem a maximum likelihood density. We call the objective of the above problem density likelihood.
