1. Why

What is the correlation between components of a normal density.

2. Result

\begin{prop} Let $g \sim \normal{\mu}{\Sigma}$. Then the correlation between $i$ and $j$ is \[ \frac{\Sigma_{ij}}{\sqrt{\Sigma_{ii}\Sigma_{jj}}} \] \end{prop}
