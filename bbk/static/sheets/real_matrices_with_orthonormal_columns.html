\section*{Matrix notation}

Let $u_1 \dots , u_k \in \R ^n$. Define $U \in \R ^{n \times k}$ so that \[ U = \bmat{u_1 & u_2 & \cdots & u_k}. \] Then $\set{u_1, \dots , u_k}$ is an orthonormal set mean \[ U^\top U = I_k \] Notice that if $k < n$, $UU^\top \neq I$, since its rank is at most $k$.
