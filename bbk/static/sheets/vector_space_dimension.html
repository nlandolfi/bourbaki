1. Why

We noticed that the number of vectors in any basis is the same.

2. Definition

Consider a vector space.

A vector space is finite-dimensional. if it has a finite basis; otherwise it is it is infinite-dimensional.

The dimension of a finite-dimensional vector space is the number of elements in any basis.
