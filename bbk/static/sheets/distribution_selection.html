1. Why

We want to summarize a dataset by a probability distribution.

2. Overview

We have a set of outcomes $A$. We have a dataset $(a^1, \dots, a^n)$ in $A$. We want to define a correspondence between the dataset and probability distributions over the set of outcomes $A$.

We call correspondences principles of distribution selection.

Notice that we do not want a distribution on the record sequences. In other words, we want a distribution on $A$ not a distribution on $A^n$.
