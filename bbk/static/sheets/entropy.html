2. Definition

The entropy of a distribution is the expectation of the negative logarithm of the distribution under the distribution.

2.1 Notation

Let $R$ denote the set of real numbers. Let $A$ be a finite set. Let $p:A \to R$ be a distribution. The entropy of $p$ is \[ -\sum_{a \in A} p(a) \log(p(a)). \] We denote the entropy of $p$ by $H(p)$.
