Let $A \in \R^{d \times d}$. The determinant of $A$ is \[ \sum_{\sigma \in S_n} \left( \sgn(\sigma) \prod_{i = 1}^{n} a_{i,\sigma_{i}} \right) \] We denote the determinant of $A$ by $\det A$.
