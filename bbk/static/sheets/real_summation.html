2. Definition

We define the sequence sum of a sequence of real numbers similar to a sequence of natural numbers.

2.1 Notation

Let $s = (x_1, \dots, x_n)$ be a sequence of real numbers. We denote the sequence sum of $s$ by \[ \sum_{i = 1}^{n} x_i \]
