1. Why

What is the additive inverse for reals.\footnote{Future editions will expand.}

2. Main Result

\begin{proposition} Let $R \in \R$. The set $\Set{-r}{r \in R \text{ and } s \not\in R}$ is an additive inverse of $R$ in $\R$. \end{proposition}

3. Notation

We denote the additive inverse of $R \in \R$ by $-R$.
