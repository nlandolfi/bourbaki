1. Why

We want to convert a subset of a vector space into a subspace.

2. Definition

The \t{span} of a subset of a vector space is the intersection of all subspaces which contain that subset. Since the intersection of a family of subspaces is a subspace, so too is the span.

A subset of a vector space \t{spans} a subspace if its span is that subspace. For example, the subspace may be a vector space, in which case the subset spans the entire space.
